Network


Ackee Finance is a project that connects various blockchain networks and supports a variety of wallets to provide a more flexible crypto asset trading experience. Below is a description of the network and what the Ackee Finance project supports.


Supported Blockchain Networks:


Ethereum


Ackee supports Ethereum, one of the most well-known blockchain networks for DeFi applications. Users can access liquidity in the Ethereum ecosystem and trade with various ERC-20 assets.

Binance Smart Chain (BSC)


Binance Smart Chain is a blockchain network that offers fast crypto transactions and affordable fees. Ackee runs on top of BSC, allowing users to utilize efficient services.

OPBNB


OPBNB is a possibly new and emerging network in the blockchain ecosystem. Ackee opens the door for users to access, providing the opportunity to explore new assets and opportunities.

Arbitrum


Arbitrum is a Layer 2 scaling solution for Ethereum. Ackee supports this network to increase transaction efficiency and reduce fees in the Ethereum ecosystem.

avalanche


The Avalanche blockchain network is also an option supported by Ackee, providing access to a variety of assets and opportunities on the network.

Polygon ZkEVM


Polygon is a Layer 2 network for Ethereum that offers high scaling and liquidity. The ZkEVM version is supported in this platform.

Scroll


Scroll is a blockchain network that aims to provide a secure and efficient decentralized infrastructure. Ackee supports Scroll, which gives users access to the network's decentralized ecosystem of crypto assets and applications.

Base


Base is a blockchain network that offers a powerful and decentralized trading experience. Ackee allows users to participate in crypto asset trading on the Base network.

Fantom


Fantom is a blockchain network known for its transaction speed and low fees. Ackee supports Fantom, which allows users to trade efficiently and cost-effectively.

Moonbeam


Moonbeam is an EVM compatible blockchain network and focuses on interoperability with the Ethereum network. Ackee allows users to participate in the Moonbeam ecosystem and run DeFi applications that communicate seamlessly with the Ethereum network.

Aurora


Aurora is a Layer 2 blockchain network that delivers a scaling solution for Ethereum. Ackee supports Aurora, which allows users to access fast and cost-effective DeFi services.

Shibarium


Shibarium is a blockchain network associated with the Shiba Inu community. Ackee supports Shibarium, which allows users to participate in this unique ecosystem and trade with Shiba Inu related assets.



Each blockchain network supported by Ackee Finance has its own characteristics and benefits, allowing users to choose the environment that suits their preferences and needs when participating in the DeFi ecosystem and trading crypto assets.

With the integration of these diverse blockchain networks, Ackee Finance provides access to a wide range of crypto assets and opportunities, enabling users to explore the potential in the rapidly growing DeFi ecosystem.

Ackee Finance continues to develop and integrate various blockchain networks to provide users with a variety of choices. This includes networks that may be new innovations in the world of blockchain.
