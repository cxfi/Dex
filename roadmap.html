Roadmap


Phase I: Launch and Initial Development


√ Smart Contract Development:
Design and develop smart contracts to facilitate decentralized liquidity and crypto asset trading.

√ Blockchain Network Integration:
Added support for several major blockchain networks and others.

√ Alpha and Beta Testing:
Test the product internally to ensure security and good performance.


Phase II: Expansion and Liquidity


√ Introduction of AMM and DAO:
Introducing Automated Market Maker (AMM) and decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) models to strengthen liquidity and community participation.

√ Partnerships:
Establish strategic partnerships with other DeFi platforms and projects to improve the ecosystem.

Phase III: Upgrade and Integration


√ Security Optimization:
Continuously improve platform security through security and technical improvements.

√ Wallet Integration:
Integrate more crypto wallets, such as Ledger, into the platform to give users more choices.

Phase IV: Community Growth and Collaboration


• Strong Community:
Recognizing the important role of the community, Ackee Finance continues to strengthen relationships with the user community. This includes community meetings, discussion forums, and collaborative initiatives.

Network Additions:
Integrating more blockchain networks, such as Avalanche, Fantom, Moonbeam, Aurora, and Shibarium.

Programs and Incentives:
Launch attractive programs and incentives for contributing community members, such as providing liquidity, joining product trials, or helping with development.

Strategic Collaborations:
Conduct strategic collaborations with other DeFi projects and platforms to strengthen the overall DeFi ecosystem.

Phase V: Future Growth and Development


Other DeFi Developments:
Introducing new DeFi products and services, such as staking, lending, and yield farming.

Market Expansion:
Pursue global expansion by adding support for more cryptocurrencies and digital assets.

Research and Innovation:
Continuously conduct research and experimentation to explore innovative blockchain technology and introduce new features.


This roadmap is Ackee Finance's commitment to providing a DeFi ecosystem and crypto asset trading.
This is a guide to the growth and development of a platform that continues to evolve according to the needs and expectations of the community and users.
