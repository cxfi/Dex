Introduction


What Ackee Finance?


Ackee Finance is a decentralized exchange center (DEX) that allows users to exchange various crypto assets without a third party and is non-custodial. utilizes Automated Market Makers (AMM) technology to adjust between 2 parties who want to exchange crypto assets. In AMM, crypto asset exchanges utilize a pool of liquidity pools filled by users. Users who store their crypto assets in liquidity pools will get incentives.

All real time transactions between users and the Platform, Ackee Finance runs using smart-contracts built on several chains that support an Ethereum Virtual Machine-based network, Ackee is built from DEX base code which allows users to transact in a decentralized manner.


F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How To Make Exchange On Ackee?

    To make an exchange you must first access the Ackee DEX platform via a browser that supports Web 3.0 and connect to it a crypto wallet, then select the asset you want to exchange and the amount, then confirm the transaction. Your assets will be immediately exchanged for the selected assets.

  • What is Meant by "Slippage"?

    Slippage is a price change that can occur between when you execute an order and when it is executed on Ackee DEX. This can happen due to rapid price fluctuations in the market.

  • What is a "Gas Fee"?

    Gas fees are fees you have to pay to process transactions on the blockchain network. This includes fees that must be paid to miners who validate transactions.

  • How to Minimize Slippage?

    You can minimize slippage by setting a price limit when you place an order. Also, using a larger liquidity pool can help reduce slippage.

  • How does Ackee maintain security?

    Ackee generally relies on smart contracts and blockchain technology for security. Additionally, users must connect their wallets, and your funds remain private.

  • Is Ackee suitable for beginners?

    Ackee can be used by beginners, but understanding the basics of crypto and security is essential. Make sure to do a basic understanding of Ackee.

  • Are transaction fees on Ackee low?

    Typically, transaction fees on Ackee are quite low. However, fees may vary depending on the blockchain network used and market conditions.
