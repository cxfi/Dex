Wallet


Various services and opportunities in the DeFi ecosystem and crypto asset trading. Wallets are one of the tools to explore the innovative, secure and decentralized world of finance.


With support for various leading blockchain networks and a variety of wallets, we give you the flexibility to trade, invest, this is the first step towards financial independence and exploration of various opportunities in the blockchain ecosystem. Here are the wallets that support it.



Supported Wallets



Metamask


MetaMask is a popular web crypto wallet that is widely used in the DeFi ecosystem. Ackee provides easy integration for MetaMask.

Coinbase


Users using Coinbase wallets can also connect with Ackee to access trading and liquidity services.

Wallet Connect


WalletConnect is a protocol that allows users to connect their wallets with DeFi applications. Ackee supports WalletConnect, enabling integration with various wallets.

Trust Wallet


Trust Wallet is a comprehensive and popular mobile wallet that supports multiple blockchain networks. Trust Wallet users can participate in Ackee.

Bitkeep


BitKeep is a mobile crypto wallet that supports access to various blockchain networks. BitKeep users can access Ackee services through this wallet.

Ledger


Hardware wallets like Ledger can also be used for additional security when interacting with Ackee.



EVM Supporting Wallets: Ackee supports a variety of wallets that are compatible with the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) network, allowing users to integrate various wallets that comply with the EVM standard.

Ackee Finance offers access to multiple blockchain networks and supports multiple wallets to provide a more flexible trading experience. This allows users to choose the environment and wallet that suits their preferences when participating in Ackee's DeFi ecosystem and trading crypto assets.
