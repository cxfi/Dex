How to Swap on Ackee Finance?


In this tutorial, we will provide guidance on how to swap crypto assets on Ackee Finance. We will use MetaMask as an example wallet.

Step 1: Make Sure You Have Crypto Assets


• Before making a swap, make sure you have the amount of crypto asset you want to exchange.

• For example, if you want to exchange ETH for USDT, make sure you have enough ETH in your wallet.


Step 2: Access Ackee DEX


• Open the Ackee Finance website in your browser and make sure you have connected your wallet.

Step 3: Select Asset Pair


• On the Ackee Finance page, select the asset pair you want to exchange.

• For example, if you want to exchange ETH for USDT, choose the ETH/USDT pair.

Step 4: Click "Add Liquidity"


• Click on the box displaying the amount of the asset you want to exchange.

• Enter the amount you want to exchange from the first asset (for example, ETH).

Step 5: Confirm and Set Amount


• Ackee Finance will display transaction details, including the amount you will receive in the second asset (for example, USDT), the gas fees charged, and the current exchange price.

• Check these details carefully.

Step 6: Confirm Transaction


• After checking the details carefully, click the "Confirm" button or something similar.

• Confirm the transaction with your wallet.


Step 7: Wait for Confirmation


• Wait until the transaction is confirmed on the blockchain network.

• The time required for confirmation depends on the level of network congestion.

Step 8: Liquidity Added


• Once the transaction is confirmed, the swap will be completed, and your assets will change according to the trade you made.



Vital Records:

Make sure you check the transaction details carefully, including any gas fees charged.

Pay attention to current exchange prices as crypto asset prices can fluctuate.

Always do your research and consideration before making a swap, especially if you are making a large trade.

By following the steps above, you can swap crypto assets easily. This allows you to access liquidity and participate in the DeFi market.
