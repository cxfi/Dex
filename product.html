Product


Ackee is a platform that combines the Automated Market Maker (AMM) and decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) models to provide a complete crypto trading experience. It is a DeFi ecosystem that allows users to participate in liquidity, exchange crypto assets, and contribute to the platform. Below is a more detailed description of Ackee Finance's features.

Decentralized Liquidity with AMM


• Ackee uses the AMM model to manage liquidity. This means that users can exchange their assets directly for liquidity provided by liquidity providers (liquidity providers) without having to wait for orders from other buyers or sellers.

• The AMM model allows users to deposit their assets in liquidity pools and obtain imbalances in a way that is automatic and proportional to the liquidity they provide.

Swap Assets


• Users can easily exchange their crypto assets across various currency pairs without needing to go to a central exchange. This works wonders in conducting trades.

Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO)


• Ackee integrates the DAO concept, which allows token holders to participate in platform decision-making. This means that the user community has control over changes and the development platform.

• The DAO allows token holders to vote, make proposals, and decide on smart contract updates and investment strategies.

Security and Transparency


• Security remains a top priority in the Ackee ecosystem. Smart contracts implemented on blockchain networks are one of the most efficient security methods, and the platform follows best practices to protect user funds. And full transparency by providing open access to smart contracts and data liquidity.

Imbalance and Profit


• Users providing liquidity can generate an imbalance in the form of platform fees and rewards. These rewards can increase their investment.


Ackee is a technology that runs on smart contracts with a blockchain network and a DeFi concept that provides decentralized trading. By combining AMM and DAO, the platform provides user experience and facilitates efficient trading in a secure and decentralized environment. This product continues to develop and contribute to the development of the DeFi ecosystem.
