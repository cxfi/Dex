    About

    Project

    It is a decentralized exchange (DEX) that allows users to exchange various crypto assets without a third party and is non-custodial, utilizing Automated Market Makers (AMM) technology to adjust between 2 parties who will exchange crypto assets in a liquidity pool that has been managed by the user.
